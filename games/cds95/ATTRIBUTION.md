# Geographic data attribution

## Natural Earth

The world map and land-mask base are derived from Natural Earth. Natural Earth data is in the public domain.

- Project: Natural Earth
- Website: https://www.naturalearthdata.com/

## GeoNames

Modern populated-place coordinates used for the V60 city-position audit are based on GeoNames data, distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 License.

- Project: GeoNames
- Website: https://www.geonames.org/
- License: CC BY 4.0

Historical sites and vanished settlements that are not represented as modern populated places are marked as `manual-historical` in `data/catalog/original-cities.json`.
