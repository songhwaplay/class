# Sculpture scan credits

The optimized GLB files in this directory are derived from the following
Scan the World models hosted by Wikimedia Commons. The source scans and these
optimized derivatives are licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0.

- `thinker.glb`: “The Thinker (Auguste Rodin)” by Scan the World / Jonathan Beck
- `david.glb`: “David (Michelangelo)” by Scan the World / Jonathan Beck
- `venus-de-milo.glb`: “Venus de Milo” by Scan the World / Jonathan Beck
- `pieta.glb`: “Pietà (Michelangelo)” by Scan the World / Jonathan Beck

License: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/
Source category: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Commons:Scan_the_World

Changes: coordinate conversion, vertex clustering, triangle deduplication,
normal regeneration, scaling, and conversion from STL to binary glTF (GLB).
